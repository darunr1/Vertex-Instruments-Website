About the product

Calculator & AI Questions

What exactly is a Vertex AI TI-84?

It's a genuine Texas Instruments TI-84 graphing calculator that has been modified by our engineering team to include a custom AI module. The module connects to ChatGPT and is accessible through the calculator's existing controls. It looks and works like a normal TI-84 — with an added layer of intelligence.

Does the AI work without internet?

The AI module requires a connection to process queries. However, all standard TI-84 functions work normally offline. The calculator never loses its original capabilities — the AI is an addition, not a replacement.

Will this void my TI-84 warranty?

Opening and modifying the hardware does void the manufacturer's warranty. However, Vertex Instruments provides its own support and quality guarantee on every unit we build, covering the modification and the original hardware.

What's the difference between Core and Pro+?

The Core model covers everyday math (algebra, stats, trig) with standard response speed and 30-day support. The Pro+ has a faster response module, expanded prompt presets (calculus, engineering), step-by-step explanations, and 90-day priority support. See the full comparison →

Using your calculator

Usage & Policy

Are these allowed in exams?

Policies vary by school and testing authority. The physical calculator is still a standard TI-84, which is allowed in most exam settings. However, AI features may not be permitted during proctored tests. Always confirm your institution's rules before using AI features in assessments.

How do I access the AI on the calculator?

The AI interface is mapped to specific key combinations on your TI-84. Your quick-start card explains exactly how to launch it, enter prompts, and navigate responses. Most users are comfortable within 5 minutes.

Does the AI drain the battery faster?

We optimize firmware to minimize battery impact. During normal mixed use (regular calculations + occasional AI queries), battery life is comparable to a standard TI-84. Heavy AI usage will drain the battery faster, similar to any connected device.

Can I use the program packs without the AI upgrade?

Yes! Program packs work on any TI-84 calculator, modified or not. They're standard TI-BASIC and Assembly programs that run natively on the device.
